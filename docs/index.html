Privacy Policy

LinkedIn Saved Posts Manager — Chrome Extension
Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

LinkedIn Saved Posts Manager is a Chrome browser extension that lets you sync, organize, and search your LinkedIn saved posts. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

The extension reads the following data solely from your LinkedIn saved posts page (linkedin.com/my-items/saved-posts) when you explicitly click the Sync button:

  • Post content (text, hashtags)
  • Post author name
  • Post URL
  • Timestamp of when you saved the post

How Your Data Is Stored

All data collected by this extension is stored exclusively on your local device using your browser's built-in IndexedDB storage. Your data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is never shared with any third party
  • Is accessible only to you through the extension

Permissions Used

  • storage — to save your posts locally in IndexedDB on your device
  • activeTab — to read your LinkedIn saved posts page only when you trigger a sync
  • linkedin.com host access — required to read post data from your saved posts page

Data Sharing

We do not collect, sell, share, or transmit any personal data to any third party — ever. There is no backend server, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by removing the extension from Chrome. All associated IndexedDB data is automatically purged when the extension is uninstalled.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
